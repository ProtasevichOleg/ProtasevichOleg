#### Frontend developer. Video Editor.

### My Personal Journey 🚀
I kicked off my Frontend development journey a little over a year ago, back in September 2022. Initially, I was all about video editing, and I loved it! But seeing people around me shifting their lives with coding got me curious. I thought it wasn’t my cup of tea until a friend of mine, who also was into video editing, introduced me to Frontend development. Oh boy, was I missing out! 😅 The realm of development opened up a playground for me. I could customize my work environment, had a broader range of tools to craft the product, and the templates... oh the templates! I absolutely adore templating repetitive tasks - it's like having your cake and eating it too! 🍰 I've got my hands dirty with HTML, CSS, and JS, and I've grown quite fond of React. It’s just amazing how it lets you build dynamic and interactive user interfaces. This learning journey has been a thrilling ride and has unveiled so many new possibilities. I’m keen on honing my Frontend skills, exploring new technologies and tools to create aesthetically pleasing and functionally efficient web solutions.
